The stomach channel  begins at just below the centre part of the lower eyelid directly below the eyebrow. From that point it moves downward in a straight line, then bends at the depression of the lower chin and moves upwards in the region in front of the ear and reaches the point 3.5 cun above the outer edge of the eyebrow.
From the depression in the lower chin where the meridian bends, via the neck, the stomach channel reaches the chest region at 4 cun from the midline of the body, then traverses 2 cun inner side towards the midline of the body in the abdominal region. Out knee, and then down towards ankle and further descends down and finally reaches at the 0.1 cun above the outer nail base of the 2nd toe.
