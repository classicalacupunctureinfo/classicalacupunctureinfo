The spleen channel  begins in the inner nail base of the big toe and runs along the inner side of the foot between two colour tones of the skin, then passes at the inner ankle joint, moves along the inner side of tibia, the inner side of the knee cap and the inner side of the thigh and reaches the groin. Then, it moves upward at 4 cun from the midline of the body at the abdominal cavity. When it reaching the chest cavity, It further deviates 2 cun more, so that it is 6 cun from the midline of the body. Then it reaches the 2nd and 3rd intercostal area, then it starts moving downward along the armpit line, ending at the depression located between the 6th and 7th intercostal area.
