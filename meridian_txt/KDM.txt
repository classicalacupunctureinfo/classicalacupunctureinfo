The kidney channel  begins in the upper region of the sole, directly below the middle toe and traverses along the inner leg and reaches the inner knee, inner thigh and reaches the abdominal region. It crosses the abdominal region at a distance of 0.5 cun from the midline of the body. When it reaches the chest cavity, it deviates outside at a distance of 2.0 cun from the midline of the body and traverses up and finally it ends between the first and second rib bones.
