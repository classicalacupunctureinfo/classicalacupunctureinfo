Then it runs along the wrist, elbow on the posterior side of the hands, then climbs across the shoulder. From there, it reaches the anterior neck and, reaches the face. Then it reaches a point between the nose and upper lip, where it crosses the midline and ends at just adjacent the nostril in the nasolabial groove.
