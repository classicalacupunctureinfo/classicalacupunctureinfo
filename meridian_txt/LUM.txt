The lung channel starts from a point at a distance of 6 cun (body inch) from the midline of the body in between the first and second rib bones in the anterior side of the body. From there, it moves upwards in the shoulders and runs along the inner region of the hands along the anterior side then it descends down to the elbow and the wrist, finally ending at 0.1 cun above the outer nail base of the thumb.
